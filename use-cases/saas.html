SaaS review management is fragmented

SaaS companies list on multiple app stores. Each has its own review system, its own rating algorithm, its own way of surfacing reviews. Checking them all means opening six browser tabs every morning.

Asking customers for reviews means pointing them to six different URLs. And when a negative review drops, you might not notice for days.

Revly brings everything together so you can stop juggling platforms and start managing your reputation from one place.

How Revly helps

Multi-Platform Monitoring

Connect all your app store listings. Revly syncs reviews from Shopify, G2, QuickBooks, WordPress, Xero, and WooCommerce automatically every 5 minutes.

Product-Centric Organization

Reviews organized by product, not scattered in a flat list. Perfect for SaaS companies with multiple products across multiple app stores.

AI-Enhanced Collection

One smart link per product. Customers share quick feedback, AI enhances it into a polished review, and they post it on the platform you choose.

Smart Platform Routing

Balance reviews across your app stores or boost your lowest-rated platform. Revly's smart routing gets reviews where they matter most.
